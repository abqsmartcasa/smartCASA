    Welcome to smartCASA

    smartCASA is an interactive web application for exploring the City of Albuquerque's progress towards meeting the requirements of the Department of Justice's Court Approved Settlement Agreement (CASA). This application breaks down the CASA into it constituent paragraphs for easy comparison against monitoring reports by the Independent Monitoring Team.
